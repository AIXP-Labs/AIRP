# AIRP Specification Overview

## Reading Guide

The AIRP specification is organized into five parts plus reserved sections:

| Part | Sections | Topic | Time |
|------|----------|-------|------|
| I | §1-§4 | Foundations: introduction, Axiom 0, definitions, principles | 15 min |
| II | §5-§7 | Communication: email transport, message types, dual-signature format | 20 min |
| III | §8-§10 | Contracts: schema (with subject/identity/escrow/invoice/dispute_resolution), lifecycle, AgentSLA | 25 min |
| IV | §11-§14 | Trust & Safety: V0-V4 dual-track, RMB payment channels, escrow account security, dispute resolution | 30 min |
| V | §15-§20 | Compliance & Reserved: prohibited commerce (incl. PRC tier-5), PIPL privacy, compliance levels, versioning, **China compliance alignment**, **cross-border (reserved)** | 30 min |

## Suggested Reading Paths

**Newcomer** (15 min): §1 → §2 → §4 → §11.2 → §12 (RMB payment)
**Implementer** (75 min): All sections in order, then ADRs
**Auditor / Compliance** (45 min): §2 → §8 → §11.2 → §13 → §14 → §15 → §16 → §19
**Cross-protocol reader** (20 min): §1 → §3.5 (stack) → §11 → §19 → ADR-003 (relationship to AIVP)
